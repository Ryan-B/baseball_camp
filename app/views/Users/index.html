Developing Skills and Frendships:

Our goal at West Seattle Baseball Camp is to build upon your camper's fundemental skills in all major facets of the game: Hitting, Fielding, Throwing, Pitching, Baserunning, Rules, Personal Behavior and Team Building.

Dynamic Interaction with Coaches:

We here at West Seattle Baseball Camp are happy to offer each camper an individual webpage that will be updated daily with images and short videos. Parents who are unable to attend camp each day will still have the opprotunity to see the daily improvement of your camper's skills.
